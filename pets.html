Pet Compendium

6 confirmed Day 1 UR pets · 11 SSR skill pets · 8 SR support pets — tiers corrected via msrwiki.com. Event pets, pull guidance, swell table, and enhancement costs from +1 to +13.

Current roster · Updated 2026 This dossier now reflects the current US server pet roster. EXR tier pets are not yet available — see § 05 for the EXR and MR tier roadmap. The quick-reference roster is also maintained in the Bible → Pets tab.

The pet system in MapleStory R is built around tiers. At launch, Fox God Throne is the source for all UR, SSR, and SR pets. Both UR and SSR pets carry active skills that fire in combat — rage skills, auto skills at various thresholds, or battle-start buffs. SR pets have no skill but contribute stats and serve as secondary enhancement fodder. UR skills are generally stronger; SSR pets also double as the primary enhancement material for pushing UR pets toward +13. As new tiers unlock, this dossier will be updated.

01All UR, SSR, and SR pets come from Fox God Throne (Pet Summon). Currency is Moonstones — shared with other banners.
02UR and SSR pets both have skills. SR pets do not. Both tiers carry rage, auto, and battle-start skills — UR skills are generally stronger. Nancy's guide misclassified several SSR pets as UR; this dossier uses tiers from msrwiki.com.
03Reaching +13 requires 12 copies of the same UR plus 9 SSR pets. Skill reaches level 14 at +13.
04EXR tier is not yet available on this server. MR tier is confirmed on SEA/JP. Both are documented in § 05 for future reference.

§ 06

Lineup and swaps

All UR equipped · situational swaps

All UR pets can be equipped simultaneously — there is no slot limit forcing a choice. The lineup question is therefore about which pets to enhance first, and which specific pets to bring to the front depending on content mode.

UR enhancement priority

Pets with undocumented skills (Bubble Fish, Jr. Necki, Ghost Pirate) are omitted until confirmed — assume lower priority until US server data is available.

1
Bone Fish Auto Skill II · 90% DMG + 0.8s stun. Universal — stun interrupts enemies in every mode (PvE bosses, Dojo mobs, PvP). Highest-impact UR to enhance first regardless of content focus.
Auto II · Stun
2
Skull Monster Auto Skill II · 44%→132% DMG + 3s poison. Highest raw Auto Skill damage ceiling among current URs. Poison DoT adds sustained pressure in PvE, Dojo, and PvP.
Auto II · Poison
3
Cellions Rage Skill · 244% AoE. Best rage-triggered pet damage. Exceptional in World Boss, PvE bosses, and Dojo. Less effective in PvP where rage timing is constrained.
Rage · AoE
4
Ribbon Pig Battle-Start · +1 initial rage. Enables rage combos one turn earlier. Meaningful in short-duration content (Dojo, PvP) where the opening burst window decides outcomes.
Battle-Start
5
Flying Fish Slime Auto Skill I · 44%→77% DMG + knock-up. Weakest Auto Skill among current URs. Knock-up has niche value in Dojo mob clears. Enhance after the four above are secured.
Auto I · Knock-up

SSR enhancement priority

SSR pets use the same slot as URs but are always active. Enhance SSRs once UR copies are secured — their battle-start and passive effects fire regardless of content mode.

SSR PRIORITY 1
Jr. Cactus

+7% Hit Rate passive. Hit rate caps determine which Dojo floors you can clear — the single most impactful SSR stat for progression.

SSR PRIORITY 2
Goby

+4.6% Critical Rate battle-start. Broadly useful — crit feeds into every damage calculation across PvE and Dojo.

SSR PRIORITY 3
Guard Robot

Auto Skill DMG increase passive. Multiplies every Auto Skill trigger — stacks well with Bone Fish and Skull Monster.

SSR PRIORITY 4
Kiyo

Rage Skill DMG increase. Synergises with Cellions — if you use rage skills frequently, this multiplies that damage directly.

SSR PvP ONLY
Cart Bear

Critical Resistance battle-start. Reduces incoming crit damage in PvP. Low value in PvE — swap in only for arena content.

§ 07

Pull guidance and copy targets

Fox God Throne · UR enhancement

Fox God Throne

The Pet Summon banner containing all UR and SR pets. Currency is Moonstones — the same pool as other summon banners. There is no separate pet-only currency.

Copy targets

+4 = 3 copies — minimum viable enhancement, activates bond stats.

+6 = 4 copies — unlocks Illustration. Pull here if you can afford it without shortchanging the next banner.

+13 = all-in — 12 same-UR + 9 SSR. Skill reaches level 14.

Do not pull 1 copy

One copy cannot reach +1 enhancement and cannot meaningfully replace any slot. Commit to at least 3 copies (+4) or save entirely. A single orphan copy is the worst spend of Moonstones.

Swell and Devour

  1. The UR Swell system feeds surplus +13 UR pets into a star progression. See § 08 for the full table.
  2. Pet Devour unlocks at player level 168 and requires the base UR to be at +13 before surplus copies can be fed in.
  3. Prioritize enhancement to +13 first. Swell and Devour are late-stage systems that reward having a fully enhanced base.

§ 09

Enhancement material costs

+1 to +13 · UR · SSR fodder from § 02 · JP server source
Source noteMaterial costs below are sourced from the Japanese server Discord (Jan–Apr 2026). EXR pets follow the same material pattern as UR when they launch. SEA/US localisation names may differ. Verify each step in-game before committing materials.

Each enhancement step consumes a specific combination of pet copies. "Same UR" means another copy of the exact same UR species; "SSR" means any SSR-tier pet. At +13 (maximum), skill level reaches 14.

EXR will follow the same pattern

When EXR launches, EXR pets are expected to use the same material structure as UR — "same UR" becomes "same EXR copy." Total investment to reach +13 EXR: 12 copies of the base EXR pet plus 9 SSR-tier pets.

Skill level at +13

Reaching +13 (maximum enhancement) unlocks skill level 14 on the pet's active skill. This is the skill ceiling and is relevant for pets whose skill effects scale with level — check the compendium entries in § 01 and § 02 for which skills have scaling descriptions.

Copy planning implication The +7–12 range costs one same-UR and one SSR per level. That is six additional UR copies across six steps on top of the copies spent reaching +6. Going from +6 to +13 requires 8 more same-UR copies and 7 SSR copies. Budget both before committing.

Bond stats

Bond stats unlock at +4 and are automatically appended to the pet's base stat line. SR/SSR/UR pets each have different bond stat tables — reference screenshots will be added when captured.
