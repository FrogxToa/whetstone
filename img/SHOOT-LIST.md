## SKIP — D-11 · Dojo Tips
The 86 images in the dojo-tips channel were **all community Q&A** (players posting their stuck floor asking for help). None are instructional screenshots. Dojo guide visuals should be purpose-shot:
- Thief S1 jump cancel position diagram → record a clip and extract a frame
- Trojan positioning → record optimal movement and extract a frame  
- These are better as short GIF/video clips than stills
