Land of Chaos and Totems

Totem spending priority, why Critical State is the key effect to target, and when to use guild coins versus farming totems from LOC directly.

Source period Sep 2023–Sep 2023 Source Elian (Top 50) · Discord #loc-totem-guide Compiled Sep 2023Filed 2026
🍄

Land of Chaos is the primary source for most totems. The question is not whether to farm LOC — it is which totem effects are worth spending guild coins to acquire directly rather than waiting for LOC drops, and which effects are secondary enough to let come naturally.

CaveatSep 2023. Totem availability in LOC and guild shops may have changed with updates. Verify before planning a coin spend.
§ 00

LOC reward structure

Source: msrwiki.com

The rewards from each LOC level shift as you progress. Understanding this validates the totem strategy below and reveals free Moonstone income most players miss.

Levels 1–120

Each level rewards Red Diamonds, Candy, and Coins. Every 4th level pays out slightly more. Red Diamond income is meaningful at early game.

Levels 121+

Per-level rewards shift to Totem Essence + Totem Fragments + Coins. LOC becomes a direct totem upgrade source from here.

Progress awards every 4 chapters (levels 4–120)

Alternates between SR pet fragments ×10 and 2 Divine Moon Stones plus 100K Coins at every 4th level. Two free Moonstones every 8 chapters is significant passive income that requires nothing except playing LOC normally.

Progress awards chapters 124–160: Silver Totem Fragments

From chapter 124, progress awards become Silver Totem Fragments (60 per award) + 200 Totem Essence. The cycle:

The Steak totem drops free as a progress reward at chapters 140 and 160. This is precisely why pushing LOC is the right path — you do not need to spend guild coins on Steak, you earn it by clearing levels.

§ 01

Spend priority: gold totem first

LOC coins

Finish gold totem resonance as the first priority

Before diversifying into other totem types, complete the gold totem resonance track. The effects unlocked here provide the foundational stat base that all other totem investments build on.

§ 02

Totem effects: what actually matters

Critical State · Stability

Two specific effects are worth understanding before deciding where to spend. CP contribution alone is not the right filter.

Steak

Critical State

Critical State: when your HP would drop to 0, you instead survive at 1% HP for 0.6 seconds. This is a combat survival mechanic, not a raw stat. Steak is one of only two totems that carry it.

Celebrity

Critical State

The second totem with Critical State. Same 0.6-second survival window. If you are building toward Critical State, both Steak and Celebrity are the targets.

Stability totems

Knockback immunity

Stability should provide immunity to knockbacks. Important for Dojo and PvP, but secondary to Critical State. Acquire these from LOC drops rather than spending guild coins directly.

Other totems

CP and stats

Most other totems contribute CP and raw stats. Valuable but not unique effects. Farm from LOC as they appear rather than prioritising coin purchases.

Why Steak despite low CP Steak's CP contribution appears small. The reason to acquire it is Critical State — which does not show up in CP at all. A 0.6-second survival window at 1% HP can determine whether you clear or wipe on a boss stage. That is worth more than the CP number suggests.
§ 03

Guild coins: when to spend

Threshold guidance
Farm Stability totems from LOC — do not buy with guild coinsDefault
Buy Stability totems with guild coins only if you reliably earn this much weekly12,000+ coins/week
The reasoning Guild coins are a limited weekly resource. Spending them on totems that drop naturally from LOC is inefficient unless you consistently generate surplus coins above the 12k/week threshold. Below that, let LOC supply the stability totems over time and spend guild coins on higher-priority items.
§ 04

Crystal totem tier

Beyond gold · JP server source
Source noteThe following is sourced from the Japanese server Discord (Jan–Apr 2026). The JP server timeline is comparable to SEA. SEA may use different localisation names. Verify in-game before committing materials.

Gold totems are not the ceiling. A Crystal tier exists above gold, introduced at higher server ages. The upgrade path requires a fully-maxed gold totem as the base material.

Gold totem Lv39 → Lv40

Final gold upgrade

Reaching level 40 is the prerequisite for crystal conversion. Materials: one gold totem of the same type consumed per upgrade step through the level 39→40 transition.

Gold Lv40 → Crystal

Crystal conversion

To convert a max-level gold totem to Crystal tier: consume one gold totem + one crystal totem. The crystal totem consumed is a separate material, not the one being upgraded.

Investment implication Completing gold totem resonance (§01) is not just a mid-game milestone — it is the entry requirement for crystal conversion. The §01 priority rule therefore holds at all stages: gold first, because crystal is gated behind it. Do not attempt crystal conversion before level 40 gold is secured.
