Off-Road Gamblers

How to allocate Off-Road Party betting tokens for guaranteed minimum coin returns, and what to spend daily Off-Road coins on.

Source Elian (Top 50) · Discord #off-road-gamblers Compiled Sep 2023Filed 2026

Off-Road Party runs twice daily (12:00 and 18:00 UTC+8) and awards Off-Road Badges through participation. The betting mechanic is optional but generates additional coins if played correctly. Elian's approach prioritises guaranteed minimum returns over chasing high multipliers.

CaveatSep 2023. Off-Road rounds, multiplier ranges, and shop contents may have changed. The principle (favour higher-multiplier rounds, split remaining tokens across all options) remains sound regardless of exact current values.
§ 01

Betting token allocation

600 tokens example

The conservative approach (Elian's default)

With 600 tokens per round, split the bet across all available options in the round offering the highest minimum multiplier. The example given: when a round offers a minimum of 5× across all options, supporting all of them is better value than other rounds offering 2× or 3× minimums.

  1. Identify the round with the highest minimum multiplier (5× example: better than 2× or 3× rounds).
  2. Spread tokens across all options in that round. At 5× minimum, this guarantees at least a 5× return on each bet placed.
  3. Take the remaining tokens (after splitting in the first round) and place them in the next-highest multiplier round — e.g. a round offering minimum 4×.
  4. Result: with 600 tokens split across both rounds this way, you are guaranteed at least 1,200 coins daily from token spending — enough to purchase at least one Moonstone from the Off-Road shop.

The aggressive alternative

Put all tokens on the single highest available multiplier. Higher ceiling, higher variance. Elian's stated preference is the conservative split; the all-in option is presented as a personal choice, not a recommendation.

§ 02

Daily shop priority

What to buy with Off-Road coins

Buy the daily Moonstone first

The Off-Road shop's Moonstone is the highest-priority daily purchase. 1 Moonstone per day compounds to 30 per month, making it a meaningful passive pull currency accumulation with no CD spend required. After the Moonstone is claimed, consider the daily packs and blue gems available in the same shop.
