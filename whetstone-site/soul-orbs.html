Soul Orbs

Fragment levelling ratios, ranking event strategy, Ephenia CD investment path, F2P shop routing, and total fragments to max a Red soul orb.

Source period Sep 2023–Oct 2023 Source Elian (Top 50), Firefighter Ulam · Discord #soul-orbs Compiled Sep–Oct 2023Filed 2026
🍄

Soul orb progression has two distinct phases: levelling for rank event scores, and long-term max investment. The fragment ratio between Ephenia and Balrog determines the most efficient path through both. Understanding the ratio and total fragment costs before committing avoids wasted resources.

§ 01

Fragment ratio: Ephenia vs Balrog

Levelling efficiency

The 1.15:1 ratio

At level 117, Ephenia requires 89 fragments per level while Balrog requires 77. The ratio is 1.15:1 (Ephenia costs 15% more fragments per level than Balrog). This ratio holds approximately through the early-to-mid levelling range.

Use this ratio for pacing: for every level you push Ephenia, push Balrog proportionally less. Once ranking events are over, resume levelling Balrog at the 1.15:1 ratio until material costs increase again, then recalculate.

§ 02

Ranking event strategy

Maximising score
  1. Level Balrog to level 50 first. This is the more efficient orb per-fragment and forms the score base.
  2. Direct all remaining fragments into Ephenia after Balrog hits 50. Ephenia's higher absolute level adds more to the ranking score.
  3. Balance the two gradually until both can be fully capped. With both Ephenia and Balrog at cap and resources managed properly, the maximum reachable score is approximately 187,677 points.
§ 03

Ephenia investment path

CD costs to reach each tier

F2P path

Via Red Gem Box during ranking event

If you unlock Ephenia from a Red Gem Box during the soul orb ranking event, the recommended F2P path is:

  1. Immediately pivot to buying Evil Stone Fragments from the shop.
  2. Work toward Soul Bloom level 2. Prerequisite: 10 total stars.
  3. Continue building Balrog Fragments as the primary spend until the star prerequisite is met.

CD investment path

Direct purchase milestones

  1. Spend 26 CD on the pop-up pack for initial efficiency.
  2. +7 Ephenia requires spending until the hidden packs appear (all-in).
  3. +11 Ephenia requires completing all 8/8 hidden packs.
  4. Grand total to reach +11: approximately 1,500 CD.
Last star reminder To complete Ephenia's final star: open all nodes AND account for the last breakthrough which requires 60 additional fragments. Do not run the calculation without including the breakthrough cost.

Swapping soul orbs

You cannot swap which soul orbs are deployed until you have levelled up your Soul Bloom. The swap mechanism requires clicking the arrays in combination. This is a prerequisite gate — plan your soul bloom levelling accordingly before expecting to be able to reorganise your deployed orb set.
