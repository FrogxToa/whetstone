Coloured Diamonds

What CDs are, why they exist, the SGD pricing tiers, and which bundles offer the best value per diamond.

Source Elian (Top 50) · Discord #how-to-use-coloured-dias Compiled Sep 2023 Filed 2026 Read 2 min

Coloured Diamonds are an alternate payment method for MapleStory R in-game packs. The core proposition is straightforward: buying CDs through the game's own CD system costs less than purchasing equivalent packs through the App Store. The base rate is fixed — one CD equals one US Dollar — but the actual price you pay depends on your local currency and which bundle tier you select.

Caveat before you read The pricing below was posted in September 2023 and reflects SGD rates at that time. Currency exchange rates and in-game pricing can change. Verify the current rates in-game before committing. The USD parity (1 CD = 1 USD) is the more stable reference point.
What are CDs

Alternate purchase currency

Coloured Diamonds let you buy in-game packs at a lower cost than paying through Apple or Google's App Store. The platform takes a cut on App Store purchases; CDs route around that cut.

Base rate

1 CD = 1 USD

The universal reference point. Everything else — SGD tiers, regional pricing — derives from this. If you are budgeting in USD, the math is simple. SGD buyers get volume-dependent rates.

§ 01

SGD pricing tiers

Value analysis

The per-CD rate in SGD is not linear — it does not simply improve with volume. The 10 CD bundle offers the best per-unit rate, followed by the 50 CD bundle. The 3 CD bundle is actually worse value per CD than buying a single CD.

Why the non-linear pricing These are likely preset bundle tiers in the game's shop rather than simply different quantities of the same item. Each tier may include bonus items alongside the CDs, which would explain why the per-CD cost does not decrease monotonically with volume. Verify what each bundle contains before purchasing on value alone.
§ 02

Where to buy

Purchase method

The original guide directs players to the #lobang channel in the Discord server for purchasing instructions. "Lobang" (Singlish for a good deal or insider tip) suggests the channel contains vendor sources or reseller options for buying CDs at favorable rates.
