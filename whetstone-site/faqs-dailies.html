FAQs and Dailies

A complete reference for daily activity priorities, red diamond math, shop spending order, and the recurring questions every player eventually asks.

Source Elian (Top 50) · Discord #faqs-dailies Additional credits @boom (Moon Mining), @Pitlord S151 (Servant Reward) Compiled Sep 2023 Filed 2026 Read 12 min

Most daily confusion in MapleStory R comes from the same cluster of questions repeated across Discord: what do I run first, where do I spend reds, what do I buy with dust, and why am I always out of gold. This dossier compiles the channel's answers into one place. Numbers are estimates from September 2023 and should be treated as reference points, not exact current figures.

Caveat before you read This channel was last captured in September 2023. Specific numbers (red dia costs per QB run, shop prices, Apex rewards) may have changed. Use the math as a framework for understanding your own economy, not as exact current values. The spreadsheet calculator at tinyurl.com/ZanBiblevolution (by Ulam, CEO) is a more precise tool — make a copy, do not request edit access.

Dimensional Trial (Adventure Team Coins)

Two phases as you progress:

  1. While missing SR characters — pick the SR reward.
  2. Once SR is maxed — pick Gold instead.
  3. Always buy the discounted Moonstones regardless of phase. Do not spend reds on anything else in this shop. Moonstones are the delayed-gratification path toward UR and EXR characters.
§ 07

Key tips and tricks

Mechanics worth knowing
Spiritual Raw Stones

Save them for Event Reset day

Whenever events refresh, a Servant Reward Up feature activates. Completing the related task on that day makes all Spiritual Raw Stones consumed yield double Secret Chest rewards until the next server reset (05:00).

Strategy: accumulate Spiritual Raw Stones across the event period, then burn them all on the Event Reset day to potentially double your Red Gem yield.

Unused stones are automatically consumed at server reset, so the only way to hold them is to keep them unclaimed in your Mail. Mail limit is 100 items — regularly delete low-value mail to maintain space.

Mail may have a 7-day expiry. Check the storage duration on your Spiritual Raw Stone mails before banking too many. Losing a stack to expiry negates the entire strategy.
Servant Reward · Credits: @Pitlord S151

Stack rewards before activating

If you finish top 32 in Duel Arena and do not claim the box, it is sent to your mail when the next Duel Arena starts. This means you can hold up to 3 reward claims simultaneously.

To maximize the Servant Reward Up bonus, activate it when you have these four reward sources ready to claim at once:

  1. World Boss rewards
  2. Duel Arena reward (unclaimed from mail)
  3. Friend Points
  4. Cash Shop Pack

Activate in the morning so the World Boss reward from your morning attack can be claimed immediately under the active bonus.

Friendship Points

Only claim from friends with 100+ intimacy

You can claim up to 50 friendship points per day. Claiming from a friend with 100 or more friendship points (intimacy) gives 2 points instead of 1. Prioritize only those friends to maximize your daily take.

The intimacy value is shown as the number next to the leaf icon in your friend list (visible via the red outline in the original guide image).

All unclaimed friendship points are cleared at 05:00 the following day. Cross-server friends count.

Run at least once daily. Whether to go beyond 3 depends on your resource tolerance; returns flatten after the first few.

Gold (when empty)

Buy the 80-reds option daily, not the one-shot max

When your gold runs dry, the temptation is to one-shot the large purchase to fill it. The recommended approach is to spend approximately 80 reds per day on the smaller purchase rather than maxing it out at once. Daily incremental buying is more efficient than a single large spend.

Totem Shop · Early season

Flying Fish Slime first, then nothing else

Before yellow totems release, sweep the discounted Flying Fish Slime Fragment. At 6k coins you get 30 pieces; 24k coins completes one free Flying Fish.

Do not buy other totem fragments from the shop — they are all obtainable from Land of Chaos. The one exception is Steak Totem fragments, which are not available there.

Server Day check

Use the Mask Event Gameplay tab

If you are unsure which day your server is on, navigate to Mask Event and open the Gameplay tab. It shows the server's current day count, which determines which content and events are available to you.
