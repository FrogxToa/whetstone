EXR Cultivation Events

How to maximize moonstone income during an EXR cultivation event, the full spend breakdown, and pull outcome math — documented from the Soul Teddy cultivation event.

Source Elian (Top 50) · Discord #exr-cultivation-guide Event EXR Soul Teddy cultivation · Sep 2023 Filed 2026 Read 4 min
Not yet availableEXR Cultivation Events have not yet launched on this server. EXR pets themselves are not yet available — see D-08 § 04 for the full EXR and MR tier roadmap. This dossier is preserved as a reference for when the event becomes available.

EXR cultivation events run for seven days and coincide with a new EXR pet banner. They are the most efficient window to accumulate moonstones because multiple parallel income streams are active simultaneously: daily prestige top-ups, event task milestones, event shop coins, and CD packs all contribute. This dossier breaks down every source from the Soul Teddy event in September 2023 and shows the pull math for a full spend scenario.

Two caveats before you read This analysis is from one specific event (EXR Soul Teddy, Sep 2023). Specific rewards from the Rainbow Run event and coin rates may differ in future events. Additionally, there is an arithmetic inconsistency in the original grand total — see the Math Discrepancy note in Section 2.

Math discrepancy — read before using this number The stated grand total of 406 moonstones does not reconcile cleanly. Adding the four line items: 127 + 35 + 162 = 324, not 406. The original post also lists "82 moonstones" from the prestige system separately before presenting the 127, but the 127 itself is broken down as 10 + 82 + 15 + 20 — meaning the 82 from prestige is already contained within the 127. Adding them again produces 406, but this appears to double-count the prestige moonstones. The defensible figure is 324 moonstones from the documented sources. Track your own event moonstones rather than relying on the 406 total.

Where the 12 EXR came from

  1. 7 copies from 525 banner pulls (702 CD worth of moonstones).
  2. 4 copies from exchanging 480 fragments in the Rainbow Escape event shop.
  3. 1 copy from winning Event Rank 1.

12 copies exceeds the +13 target (which requires 14 copies: 1 base + 13 enhancements). A second full-spend event would push toward a maximally enhanced EXR. This outcome was achieved starting from 0 pre-pities.

Event rank 1 is not reliable The +1 EXR from ranking first is not a guaranteed outcome. Competition for top rank is server-dependent and heavily P2W. Do not factor rank prizes into minimum planning targets.
§ 04

Applying this to future events

General principles

Always hit the task milestone

Regardless of spend tier, completing event tasks through the final milestone is the highest-priority action during any cultivation event. The free moonstone yield (estimated 10–20 MS from tasks alone) is accessible to all players and does not require CD spending.

The 26 CD pack is the best value entry

35 moonstones for 26 CD works out to approximately 1.35 moonstones per CD — significantly better than converting CD to blue diamonds and buying moonstones that way. If only one paid pack is accessible, the pop-up pack is the one to prioritize.

Event shop fragments compound the outcome

The 4 copies from the event shop (480 fragments) were a major contributor to the 12-copy total. Actively farming event-specific fragments during cultivation weeks adds EXR copies at no additional CD cost beyond what the event itself provides.

Prestige top-up timing matters

The prestige reward requires hitting 600 prestige per day for 5 days and reaching 3,000 accumulative — best executed during an event week when the moonstone return justifies the CD spend. Outside of events, the same prestige spend returns fewer high-value rewards.
