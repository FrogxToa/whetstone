Dossier No. 03

Event Timeline

A full week-by-week log of cultivation events, shop additions, and server day unlocks — from Week 1 through Week 37 (Day 253). Use this to plan which fragments to stockpile before the next event cycle.

Source Elian (Top 50), Firefighter Ulam, EvilHelen · Discord #event-timeline Period Sep 2023 – Apr 2024 Server days D1 – D253 Filed 2026 Read 8 min

Cultivation events are the primary vehicle for earning ranking rewards and titles in MapleStory R. Each event asks you to invest specific fragments or materials to accumulate points; higher totals place you in better reward tiers. Knowing the rotation in advance lets you time your fragment spending rather than burning stockpiles between events.

Caveat before you read This timeline was logged on one server from Week 1. Later servers may follow the same rotation in the same order, or the rotation may shift. Treat weeks 1–10 as a reliable early-game reference and later weeks as indicative rather than guaranteed. Confirm the current event in-game before spending saved fragments.
01Events rotate through Soul Orb, Wings, Stigmata, Pet, Mental Link, Spirit — roughly every 7 weeks for each.
02Red fragments score 10 points. Orange fragments score 5. Save reds for orb/soul weeks.
03Not all fragments count. Each week specifies exactly which items score points.
04Day 65 is a notable server milestone: special Abyss event unlocks.
05Day 99 introduces Mental Link competition, Bumper Chess, and El Nath Training.

§ 01

Event rotation patterns

What to expect

After 37 weeks of data, a clear rotation emerges. The cycle is not rigid — special events and re-runs interrupt it — but the broad pattern holds. Understanding it lets you decide which fragments are safe to spend between events.

Appears every ~7 weeks

Soul Orb

Weeks 5, 10, 14, 18, 22, 26, 30, 34. Red fragments score 10 pts, Orange 5 pts. Specific orbs called out each time — only those count.

Appears every ~8 weeks

Wings

Weeks 3, 7, 11, 16, 24, 28, 32, 36. Each event names which wing tiers count. Later events specify Red (10pts), Gold/Yellow (3pts), Orange (5pts).

Appears every ~7 weeks

Stigmata

Weeks 4, 8, 12, 20. Specific stigmata named each time. Later events include Vampire Instinct, Prom Mask, Black/White Yaksha.

Appears every ~6 weeks

Pet (EXR/UR)

Weeks 6, 9, 13, 17, 21, 25, 29, 33, 37. Each cycle introduces a new EXR pet and adds the previous cycle's EXR to the Pet Banner and ANE Dust shop.

Introduced Week 15

Mental Link

Weeks 15, 19, 23, 28, 35. Each fragment gives 10 points. Introduced alongside Bumper Chess and El Nath Training at Day 99.

Introduced Week 27

Spirit Training

Weeks 27, 31, 35, 39. Only Red fragments count. Red Spirit gives 480 pts, Red fragment gives 12 pts. Later addition to the rotation.

Fragment timing advice If you are holding a large stockpile of Soul Orb or Wings fragments, check the current event type first. Spending the week before your category's event wastes the points they would have scored. The event after your category just ended is the safest window to convert excess fragments.
§ 02

Server day milestones

Permanent unlocks

These are additions that unlock at a specific server day and remain available afterward. Different from weekly events — these change the permanent shop and feature landscape.

D65
Special Abyss Event

One-time event tied to server reaching Day 65. The more Abyss runs you complete, the more rewards you earn. Noted as a notable milestone by both Elian and Ulam. Specific pack contents credited to @Haenjin.

D90
Crystal Totem Fragment added to Adventure Team Shop

Also: 2nd last day of Pet Ranking Event. Crystal Totem Fragment Weekly Daily Pack introduced.

D94
Arkarium added to Red Gem Shop

Ephenia and Netherworld Monk Fragments added to Auctions. Riding Mount introduced. Secret of Riding Pet Cultivation Pack available in Diamond Shop (weekly 3×, 15 CD each).

D96
Ephenia Fragments (×10) added to Blue Diamond Shop

Important F2P milestone for players building Ephenia without spending Red Gems.

D99
Mental Link Cultivation Competition introduced

Also introduces Bumper Chess and El Nath Training game modes. Major content expansion that adds a new cultivation category to the rotation.

D102
Earliest possible Mental Link Auto Skill III unlock

Requires Snow Field Common Soldier IV rank and every Frigid Snow Bones available up to that point. Not achievable before D102 regardless of investment.

D106
Exoskeleton Wing Fragment added to Duel Arena Shop

Ghost Pirate Viking added to More Wish feature. Ethereal Branch of Flowering Leaf Fragment added to Body ANE shop and general Shop.

D113
EXR Buffoon added to Pet Banner and Body ANE Dust shop
D120
Ephenia Orb added to Tower of Origins shop

Notable for F2P players farming TOO currency — Ephenia becomes directly purchasable.

D134
Vampire Stigmata added to Dust shop

Prom Mask Stigmata added to Arena shop. Pink Doodle Wings added to Arena shop.

D148
Von Leon (Red) Soul Orb introduced

Administrator Rousseau (Orange) introduced. Arkarium added to Red Gem Shop and ANE dust Exchange.

D191
Calls from Abyss 10 introduced
D204
Cygnus Orb (Rainbow tier) introduced

Holy Land Beast added to Origin shop. Von Leon added to ANE dust Exchange. Rainbow is a new tier above Red for soul orbs.

D253
MR tier pet introduced (Crocky the Gatekeeper)

MR is a new pet tier above EXR. Events now count MR, EXR, and UR pets/fragments. EXR Birk added to Pet Banner and ANE dust Exchange.
