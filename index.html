§ 03

House standards

Why trust this
I.

Skeptical first

No claim is repeated because it sounds confident. Source dates are visible. Statements like "never touch this" are flagged for in-game verification rather than mirrored as gospel.

II.

Sourced, not invented

Guides cite the player, post date, and channel they were drawn from. Where the original advice has aged, the dossier says so and explains what is likely to have shifted.

III.

Unofficial, on purpose

No affiliation with Nexon, the developer, or any sponsor. Whetstone is reader-funded curiosity. If a guide ever paywalls, it will say so. None do today.
